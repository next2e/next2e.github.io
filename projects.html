All-Wing (pardon the stock photos):

2ArcadE

2 East's very own homemade arcade machine! The various assortment of knowledge and skills of many 2 Easterners (Easters? we aren't holidays, idk) came together to design, build, program, and decorate an arcade machine in summer/fall 2016.

Led by Emmanuel Fasil and Harry Thaman.

Furniture Boiz

2E's build group. Their most recent project, Bar-enda, is designed and built to house two and a half shelves of liquids, as well as a 2ft cube minifridge (truly a staple piece of furniture for all your partying needs). Members are also involved in build projects like Next Big Thing (big things in the courtyard) and Next Haunt (an escape-the-room haunted house in the basement).

Built by multiple 2 Easters, co-designed by Emmanuel Fasil and Brenda Stern.

LED wall

2E is lit, and as such, its lights must keep up with itself. Justin is building a wall to mount his LEDs for the enlightenment of the wing - and he's going to make 2 West pay for it! Just kidding, 2E welcomes outsiders. Come see the 2E light show! :)
